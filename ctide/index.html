What is Dragon Boating ?

Who we are and what we do

[TOP]

Dragon boat is a team-based paddling sport. With a full boat of twenty members, we train our technique, speed, and synergy. Over the course of the year, we attend many boating races called regattas.

We hold a number of practices a week concentrating on increasing overall physical fitness and having tons of fun at the same time. Not only do we work hard but we play hard too. Throughout the year we have clubbing/pubbing events, board game nights, movie nights, socials, parties, etc. This isn't just any sports team - it's a huge social club. So come out, meet new people and get in shape. Our team runs from November through till late July.

What to Expect

Key Experiences you'll have this year

[TOP]

Twice Weekly Practices

Outer Harbor Marina

On the water at the Outer Harbor Marina, we train our technique, speed, and synergy. We meet twice weekly and practice for two hours.

Top of the line Training

Afterburn Fitness

Andrew, an UTSC Alumni and owner of Afterburn Fitness sponsors Crimson Tide. We recieve coaching and training from top-of-the-line staff.

Lively Dragon Regatta

Milton

The Milton Regatta hosted by Lively Dragon is a large event that happens in Mid-May. It's a fun day filled with 200m, 500m, and 2000m races.

Toronto International Dragon Boat Race Festival

Center Island

One of the largest Dragon Boat events in Canada. This festival brings some of the best teams from accross the world together for a weekend of fun.
